Portal Siege

Portal Siege (formerly "MergeAutobattler") is a mobile-first autobattler where you merge units, build an army, and survive escalating waves of enemies. The game is built with Angular and Pixi.js and is available as a web build and an Android app (packaged with Capacitor).

Game Overview

Portal Siege is a mobile strategy game that combines the addictive loop of merge mechanics with the intensity of wave-based combat. Collect and merge units to evolve a powerful army, then deploy them against relentless enemy waves that escalate with every round.

Careful positioning, timely upgrades, and smart merging decisions are the keys to defeating increasingly dangerous bosses and pushing deeper into enemy territory. How far can you go?

Game Modes

Portal Siege supports multiple playstyles: story-driven missions and infinite survival runs.

Story Mode

Play curated missions and chapters with scripted waves, a story shop, and locked research nodes that unlock progression. Early missions use "elite" normal enemies (HP multipliers) for readable difficulty without complex boss mechanics.

Infinity Mode (Quick Run)

Survive endless, escalating waves. This mode focuses on long-term strategy and resource efficiency—how far can you push your army?

Research Systems

Two complementary research systems are available: Run Research (session-only bonuses applied during a run) and Permanent Research (meta upgrades that persist between runs, e.g., bench slots, starting gold, summon-cost changes, and castle HP bonuses).

Features

Core systems and quality-of-life features drawn from the game's source.

🔗

Merge Engine

Match two units of the same type and tier to create a stronger unit — the primary progression loop.

🧭

3×3 Battle Grid

Battles resolve on a compact grid; positioning matters — tanks hold, rogues flank, archers snipe from behind.

🪑

Bench & Staging

Use the bench to hold spare units and plan merges without clogging the active board before a wave.

✨

Permanent Research

Meta progression unlocks long-term upgrades between runs to smooth early play and expand strategies.

💰

Summons & Economy

Spend gold to summon units and feed the merge engine — resource timing is part of the strategy.

🔁

Save / Transfer

Export and import save data to move progress between devices.

How to Play

Quick tips to get started and improve faster.

Merge

Combine identical units to upgrade them — merging saves board space and is key for late waves.

Grid & Bench

Only the 3×3 active grid fights. Use the bench to stage future merges and avoid cluttering the board.

Summons

Spend gold to summon — prioritize value and timing over impulse purchases.

Research

Choose research that complements your run: army power, castle survival, control, or economy.
