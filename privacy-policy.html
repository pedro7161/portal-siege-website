Overview

🔒 Privacy First

Portal Siege is committed to protecting the privacy of its players. This policy explains what information we handle (and what we do not) when you play the game on your mobile device.

The short version: we do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data Storage

Your game progress — including levels completed, upgrades unlocked, and settings — is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external server.

If you uninstall the game, locally stored data may be removed by your operating system according to its standard app-data policies.

Data Collection

Portal Siege does not collect any personal information, including but not limited to:

• Name, email address, or other contact information
• Device identifiers or advertising IDs
• Location data
• Usage statistics or gameplay telemetry

Third-Party Services

Portal Siege currently does not integrate any analytics platforms, advertising networks, crash-reporting tools, or other third-party services that could collect data about you.

Should this change in a future update, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly and users will be notified through the app store listing.

Children's Privacy

Because Portal Siege does not collect any personal information from anyone, it is safe for players of all ages, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children or any other users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy if the game adds new features that affect data handling. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "last updated" date.

We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed. Continued use of the game after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
